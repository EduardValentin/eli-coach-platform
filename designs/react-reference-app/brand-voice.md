# Brand Voice

Our brand voice is the foundation of how we connect with women seeking personal training and hormonal balance. It must always sound personal, human, empowering, supportive, and confident.

## Tone & Vibe
- **Personal & Human:** Speak as if you are a knowledgeable friend or mentor.
- **Empowering & Supportive:** Encourage the user, acknowledge their struggles, and provide clear, actionable steps.
- **Confident:** Be an authority in fitness and cycle-syncing without being arrogant.

## Rules
- **NEVER** sound like generic AI-generated text.
- **AVOID** overly used buzzwords such as:
  - "Unlock your potential"
  - "World-class" / "best-in-class"
  - "Seamless experience"
  - "Leverage" (in any non-financial context)
  - "Transformative"
- Limit exclamation marks (max one per page).
- No emojis in copy text.

## Examples

**Do this:**
> Start feeling better today — it only takes a few minutes.
> You're one step away from figuring this out.
> Pick up where you left off.
> Let's build a plan you can actually stick to.

**Not this:**
> Unlock your full potential with our world-class workout programs.
> Empower yourself through transformative educational experiences.
> Begin your journey toward fitness excellence today.
